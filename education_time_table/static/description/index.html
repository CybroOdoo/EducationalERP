Overview

Educational ERP is a one-stop shop for all your school administration management activities. Timetable management module enables you to efficiently manage the academic year via streamlining the timetable keeping focus on all subjects and their management. Timetable management module helps in aligning the proper schedule and thereby allot faculties as per their availability. It delivers high flexibility in restructuring the periods in accordance to the need. One can easily sort the timetable according to the classes, their division or with respect to the faculties. Time management module acts as robust platform to forecast and thereby plan accordingly the efficient utilization of school time resulting greater performance and productivity.
